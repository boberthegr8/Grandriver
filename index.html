About Brad

Owner, mechanic, and the guy handing you the key

Brad grew up a few hundred metres from where the Grand meets Erie. His family kept a boat at Port Maitland before he could walk, and he's been on this water every summer since — first on his dad's ski boat, then on whatever ski he could keep running through a season.

He's spent his working life around personal watercraft: wrenching on them, running them, and towing people back in when a weekend rider gets in over their head. Wet N Wild came out of that — a small fleet he maintains himself, so he knows exactly what shape every machine is in when it leaves the dock.

"If it isn't right, it doesn't go out. That's the whole business model."
